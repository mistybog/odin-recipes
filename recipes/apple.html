Apple Tart

Description

A crisp apple tart hits the spot after a long day of fishing and bug-catching. This sweet recipe gives the player five energy points, to be used when breaking rocks or relocating trees.

Ingredients

  • Flour (1)
  • Sugar (1)
  • Apple (1)

Where to Obtain Recipe

  1. From a villager while they are cooking in their house
  2. In a message bottle on the beach
  3. The chef at the restaurant in Happy Home Paradise
  4. After designing a vacation home for Apple and Hornsby

Further information on this recipe can be found at Nookipedia.
